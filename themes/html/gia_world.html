By Malene Birger is happy to announce the opening of the newly renovated flagship store located at Marylebone High Street, London.

“With an impeccable aesthetic eye and an extreme focus on details, Dimore Studio turned out to be the perfect match for By Malene Birger. The new concept is going to be remarkably different thus still very much in line with the brand’s heritage. Our customers are constantly developing, so must By Malene Birger and we are thrilled to take this journey with Emiliano Salci and Britt Moran”, says By Malene Birger’s Chief Executive Officer Charlotte Egelund.

The Concept
The London branch is the second By Malene Birger flagship store to undergo a complete renovation. The concept is a classic take with a modern feel, a focus on custom-built and handcrafted elements, walls featuring the brand´s signature elements and chandelier in the emblematic monochrome colors of the fashion house.

“The inspiration for the new By Malene Birger store concept came from the idea of an all-female world” according to Emiliano Salci and Britt Moran – “especially in terms of shape and colors. An environment softened with circular shapes, curved lines, enveloping lights and warm colors enhanced by the use of leather” add the designers of DIMORE STUDIO – “that makes the selection and the fitting of the product enjoyable.”
